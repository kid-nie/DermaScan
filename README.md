# dermascan-
AI-powered application that helps users analyze makeup and skincare product ingredients so that they can purchase the best suited product for their skin type/condition. (AI Makeup Ingredient Checker)

## Problem
Many people struggle to understand long cosmetic ingredient lists and may unknowingly use products that cause irritation or acne.

## Solution
This app allows users to upload ingredient images or text and uses AI to analyze and summarize whether a product is safe based on skin type and preferences.

## Key Features
- Upload ingredient images/ pastes ingredients
- AI extracts and reads text
- Analyzes every ingredient to point out which is beneficial, harmful or even neutral in effect
- Summarizes product safety
- Personalized recommendations
